Refund Policy

Cancellation-and-Refund

You have a period of one month to use this software on zero subscription fee basis. Once you are certain that you want this data management software for your business, then only you may subscribe. After you have made the payment for subscription, there will be a period of 7 days to cancel your subscription by mailing your request to studionomy4u@gmail.com as well as through a button provided on your profile page. A telephonic conversation may be done to ensure the authority of the request sender and obtain your feedback regarding the website and usage.

Orders paid by Credit/Debit Card will be refunded by credit back to the Credit/Debit Card within 14 working days and the refund will reflect in the next statement.

Orders paid by net banking accounts will be credited back to bank account.

For all other modes of payment, we will send a refund cheque. The cheque will be made in favour of the name as in the "billing name" provided at the time of placing the order.

Post refund, you will not be able to access your user ID and data submitted by you during your usage. The data will remain saved in the server for 1 year after which STUDIONOMY holds the right to delete it.

If you want to renew your subscription within one year of cancellation, then you get all the data you have submitted in the website. If you renew your subscription after passing of one year since cancellation, STUDIONOMY cannot be held responsible for recovery of your data.
