About Us

Studionomy is a software to manage Photography Studios and similar workplaces. This is a beautifully designed website exclusively tailored to the daily needs of a photography studio.

  • Quotation Building
  • Job Entries
  • Inventory Management
  • Employee Records
  • Billing
  • Dynamic Calendar
  • Multiple Accounting

But thats not all. We are also providing a strong networking platform amongst Studios , Free Lancers and Equipment Owners.

Freelancers and Equipment-Owners will have their independent shareable profiles. Here free lancers can showcase their photographic and cinematographic talents through social media links. Equipment-Owners will be able to share their equipment inventory for renting.

Most importantly calendars of Studios, freelancers and equipment owners are dynamically inter-connected. You need not ever maintain any other records for your job search, job bookings, dates, equipment for various jobs, billing and payments.

Last but not the least, we encourage you to be a part of this amazing platform with very attractive price packages, exclusively fabricated for all your needs.

We also encourage our members to share their feedback and valuable suggestions with us to improve further based on your requirements.
